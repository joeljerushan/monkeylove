MonkeyLove ($MLV) is a community-driven meme coin inspired by the legendary Monkey Love & Coconut Crisis in Sri Lanka. The project is designed to bring humor, engagement, and real-world crypto utility to its holders while rewarding the community for their support and participation. 🚀🐵🥥

2. Vision & Mission

🌍 Vision

To create a fun, engaging, and rewarding ecosystem where users can earn, trade, and enjoy MonkeyLove ($MLV) as a leading meme coin in the crypto space.

🔥 Mission

  • • Create Play-to-Earn (P2E) games and NFTs powered by $MLV
